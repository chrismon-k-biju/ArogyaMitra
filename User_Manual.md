# ArogyaMitra User Manual

Welcome to the ArogyaMitra platform! ArogyaMitra is a comprehensive healthcare companion application built to provide secure, structured, and accessible health services. It connects migrant workers, healthcare providers, and public health officials through tailored portals, simplifying health record management, insurance claims, public health analytics, and disease outbreak reporting.

This manual outlines the features and workflows for each role within the system.

---

## 1. Migrant Worker Portal

**Target Audience:** Migrant workers seeking access to health records, medical camps, and insurance claims.

### Features
* **Multilingual Support:** The interface supports multiple languages (English and Hindi), allowing users to switch easily to their preferred language right from the start.
* **Registration & Health ID:** Workers can self-register using their phone number, full name, age, originating state and district, and occupation. Upon successful registration, a unique Health ID and a scannable **QR Code** are generated.
* **Migrant Dashboard:**
  * **Digital Health Profile:** View medical history and essential personal details linked to the Health ID.
  * **Medical Records:** Securely view all medical records (e.g., General Checkups, Lab Results, Prescriptions) added by healthcare providers.
  * **Insurance Claims:** Check the status of treatment cost claims submitted by doctors during visits.
  * **Emergency Contacts:** Manage an emergency contact number from the profile page.
  * **Health Camps & Alerts:** Access information on upcoming health camps and receive real-time disease or vaccination alerts mapped to current districts.

---

## 2. Healthcare Provider Portal

**Target Audience:** Doctors, Nurses, and Health Workers operating in clinics or out in the field.

### Features
* **Role-Based Access Control:** Secure and specialized dashboard views tailored for Doctors, Nurses, and Health Workers.
* **Doctor Dashboard:**
  * **Patient Appointments:** View a daily schedule of patients ("Today's Patients") sorted by time and visit type.
  * **Medical Record Upload:** Search for patients using their unique Health ID to upload digital health records (prescriptions, checkups, lab results).
  * **Auto-Generated Insurance Claims:** When entering treatment costs during a consultation, an automatic insurance claim is generated and routed to health workers for processing.
  * **Camp Management:** Start and manage targeted health sessions in "Camp Mode" specifically for large-scale worker screening.
* **Health Worker Dashboard:**
  * **Field Registration:** Register migrant workers on-site via the "Register Migrant Worker" module to ensure inclusion of unconnected individuals.
  * **Public Health Reporting:** Actively report immediate public health concerns (e.g., localized disease outbreaks or mass vaccination counts) through the "Report Public Health Alert" tool.
  * **Claim Processing:** Review, approve, or reject auto-generated treatment insurance claims based on internal policy metrics.

---

## 3. Public Health Portal

**Target Audience:** Government Health Officials, District Medical Officers, and Administrators.

### Features
* **Secure Official Login:** District-level authentication for district-specific monitoring and control.
* **Analytics Dashboard:**
  * **Real-time Statistics:** Monitor active disease cases/alerts and total vaccination rates aggregated from reports generated by health workers across the district.
  * **Active Alert Monitoring:** View priority alerts flagged as "High" for diseases and "Log" for vaccines.
* **Administrative Controls:**
  * **Health Report Generation:** Auto-generate and download official analytical health reports outlining district statistics in a downloadable text format.
  * **Release Circulars:** Draft and broadcast department-wide health circulars directly to all healthcare providers in the selected district.
  * **Provider Management:** Dedicated functionality to "Create Provider" and "Manage Providers," facilitating the onboarding of new Doctors and Health Workers to the ArogyaMitra system.

## General Instructions

* **Data Security:** ArogyaMitra ensures state-of-the-art data integrity. Healthcare records are tied exclusively to the secure Health ID ecosystem and are confidential.
* **System Connectivity:** The mobile application relies on consistent connectivity with its centralized backend server to relay notifications, auto-process claims, and maintain real-time database synchronicity.
* **QR Codes:** Always encourage migrant workers to save their generated Health ID QR code. It can be easily presented/scanned at medical camps for instant record retrieval without manual searches.
